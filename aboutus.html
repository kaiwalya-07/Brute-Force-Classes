Mission Statement

Our mission is to provide our students with the best learning experience possible. We do this by offering the most up to date and comprehensive eLearning courses available. We aim to make it easier for our students to learn. We aim to make our courses as affordable as possible. We aim to provide our students with the best customer service possible. We are always open to new ideas, improvements and suggestions on how we can better serve our students. We are always looking for new ways to stay current with technology, so that we can deliver the best possible experience to our students.

To provide students with a platform to learn basic computer skills, including how to use a word processor, spreadsheet, presentation software, and email. Students can also learn about Microsoft Office, Internet basics, and research skills. In turn, this will provide students with the knowledge to do well in other classes and in the workplace.
